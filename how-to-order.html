How to Order Chitii Mangoes

Ordering Instructions

To place an order for Chitii Mangoes, please follow the steps below:

  1. Select the quantity of mangoes you want to order.
  2. Join our WhatsApp group to stay updated: Join WhatsApp Group
  3. Provide your delivery details in the WhatsApp group.
  4. Wait for confirmation of your order.

Delivery Areas

We deliver to the following areas:

  • Area 1
  • Area 2
  • Area 3

Delivery Timings

Our delivery timings are as follows:

  • Monday to Friday: 9 AM - 6 PM
  • Saturday: 10 AM - 4 PM
  • Sunday: Closed

Packaging Information

Chitii Mangoes are carefully packed to ensure they reach you in perfect condition, typically in eco-friendly packaging.
